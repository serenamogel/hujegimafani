    Her father’s name is Anirban Sen, and her mother’s name is Rajrupa Sen. She is the only child of her parents.

    Career

    She started her career in 2009 with Zee TV’s serial ‘Yahaan Main Ghar Ghar Kheli.’ In 2012, she was featured in the music video ‘Humko hai aasha.’ In the same year, she appeared in the TV serial ‘Baal Veer,’ in which she played the character of Meher.

    Anushka Sen in Baal Veer

    In 2015, she acted in the Bollywood film ‘Crazy Cukkad Family.’

    Anushka Sen in Crazy Cukkad Family

    In 2018, she appeared in the TV serials like Internet Wala Love and Devon Ke Dev…Mahadev. She was roped in for the Colors TV’s serial ‘Jhansi Ki Rani’ along with Vikas Manaktala in 2019.

    Anushka Sen as Jhansi Ki Rani

    In the same year, she acted in a period drama film ‘Lihaaf: The Quilt,’ and did a short film ‘Sammaditthi.’

    Anushka Sen in a Short Film

    She is a popular TikTok star and social media influencer. She created sponsored videos for UrbanClap, KOOVS, and Myntra.

    She was featured in many music videos including ‘Gal Karke’ (2018), ‘Viah’ (2018), and ‘Superstar’ (2020).

    She has also appeared in various TV commercials.
