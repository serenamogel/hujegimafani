    Burna Boys Love, Damini Album Dominates Apple Music Top 100

    The African Giant on Friday, July 8, 2022 served the music community with his hugely anticipated studio album labeled; Love, Damini. The nineteen tracked album since its release has become the topic on social media.

    30 Aug 2024, 00:00

    Grammy award winning singer and performer, Damini Ebunoluwa Ogulu, popularly known as Burna Boy continues his rapid rise to the top of African music royalty.

    The African Giant on Friday, July 8, 2022 served the music community with his hugely anticipated studio album labeled; “Love, Damini’. The nineteen tracked album since its release has become the topic on social media.

    The star-studded studio body of work features highly rated recording artiste in the entertainment scene including; Ladysmith Black Mambazo, J Hus, Victony, Popcaan, Kehlani, Blxst, Ed Sheeran, J Balvin and Khalid.
