    LaTavia Roberson is an American singer, songwriter, and actress. She is best known as a founding member of the R&B girl group Destiny's Child. She was born on November 1, 1981 in Houston, Texas. Roberson began her career in music as a member of the R&B girl group Girl's Tyme, which later became Destiny's Child. The group released their debut album, Destiny's Child, in 1998. The album was a success, spawning the hit singles "No, No, No" and "Bills, Bills, Bills". Roberson left the group in 2000, citing creative differences. She then pursued a solo career, releasing her debut album, LaTavia, in 2002. The album was not as successful as her work with Destiny's Child, and she eventually returned to the group in 2004. Roberson has also appeared in several films and television shows, including the films The Fighting Temptations and ATL. She has also appeared on the reality show Celebrity Fit Club. Roberson is currently working on her second solo album, which is set to be released in 2021. She is also working on a memoir, which is set to be released in 2022.
