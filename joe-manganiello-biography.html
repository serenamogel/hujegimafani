    Who is Joe Manganiello?

    Joe Manganiello is an American actor, director, and producer. He is also a philanthropist and author. He is known for his role as Flash Thompson in Sam Raimi’s Spider-Man trilogy.

    Joe is listed in one of Men’s Health’s ‘100 Fittest Men of All Time’. He has more than 40 credits as an actor for his appearances in numerous movies and television series.

    Joe Manganiello: Birth Facts, Family, Childhood

    Joe was born in Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania on December 28, 1976, to Charles John Manganiello and Susan of Italian, Dalmatian Croatian, and Armenian heritage.

    He has a brother named Nicholas Manganiello and they were raised in Mt. Lebanon, Pennsylvania.

    In which school did Joe Manganiello attend?

    Joe attended St. Bernard School and Mt. Lebanon High School and graduated in 1995. He was the captain of his football, basketball, and volleyball teams growing up and even went on to play at the varsity level in all three sports.

    He later joined the University of Pittsburgh and while studying at the University, he traveled to New York and Los Angeles to participate in numerous group auditions.

    Joe Manganiello: Professional Life, Career

    Joe was involved in numerous productions while he was enrolled at Carnegie Mellon University. He was involved in Pittsburgh’s theater scene in Henrik Ibsen’s ‘When We Dead Awaken’. After graduation, he moved to Los Angeles and signed with a talent agent.

    Although Joe’s initial acting roles are not recognized, he gained fame after appearing as Eugene “Flash” Thompson in the Sam Raimi-directed film ‘Spider-Man’. He has since then appeared in numerous movies and television series in his ongoing acting career.

    Some of the movies and television series that he has been part of include ‘The Ketchup King’, ‘Jake in Progress, ‘CSI: Crime Scene Investigation’, ‘Las Vegas, ‘Close to Home’, ‘So Notorious’, ‘American Heiress’, ‘Scrub’, ‘ER’, ‘Impact Point’, ‘CSI: Miami’, ‘Irene in Time’, ‘Medium’, ‘CSI: NY’, ‘‘til Death’, ‘One Tree Hill’, ‘100 Questions’, ‘Livin’ on a Prayer’, ‘Two and a Half Men’, ‘White Collar’, ‘What to Expect When You’re Expecting, ‘How I Met Your Mother’, ‘Neil’s Puppet Dreams’, ‘True Blood’, ‘Knight of Cups’, ‘Tumbledown’, ‘Magic Mike XXL’, ‘Blaze and the Monster Machines, ‘Mom’ and more.

    Lifetime Achievement, Nominations

    Joe won the Saturn Award in the category of Best Guest Starring Role on Television in ‘True Blood’. He was also nominated for MTV Movie Awards and has also won Scream Awards. He was also nominated for Teen Choice Awards.
