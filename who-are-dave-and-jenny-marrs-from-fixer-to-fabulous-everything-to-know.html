    Dave And Jenny Marrs are reality Tv personalities from America. They are widely known as the couple who host the HGTV program Fixer to Fabulous and are famous for their home renovation videos.

    The Tv show is about a couple restoring old homes in their surroundings. They fix everything from ruined porches to rusty attics and give the historic homes a new make-over. This is why the show is named Fixer to Fabulous.

    Husband Dave is a builder, and his wife Jenny is a designer and writer. They are a perfect duo for the show. Jenny always loved decorating houses, but it only became a passion when Dave started working as a builder.

    The pair has renovated more than 300 homes together. They have created happiness and forever homes for many people.

    How Old Are Dave And Jenny Marrs?

    Dave is currently 41 years. He was born on February 26, 1980. His wife Jenny is 42 and was on January 24, 1979.

    In 2022, after Dave graduated from college, he started working for the construction company Rubbermaid from Newell Brand. During his time in Rubbermaid, he met Jenny, and the two became close.

    The couple had a long-distance relationship for quite some time as Dave was frequently traveling due to his job. Later both of them decided to tie the knot.

    After some time, Dave got sick of his job and left the company. He grew up in Colorado building tiny wooden homes with his father and wanted to be a builder. Jenny trusted Dave and took a giant leap of faith. The couple decided to try renovating houses for a few years in Arkansas.

    Dave And Jenny Marrs: Children & Family

    Jenny has had infertility issues for quite some time. The pair has adopted four children. One of the children, Sylvie, was adopted from DPR Congo. Their other children are named Luke, Charlotte, Nathan, and Ben.
