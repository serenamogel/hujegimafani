    Raquel Moore Career

    Moore is best known for being in the television series, The Amazing Race 33. Moreover, she was featured alongside her fellow Flight Attendant, Cayla Platt. Early on, the flight attendants warned other racers not to underestimate them and proved to make the most of this claim. They were also granted greater motivation post-restart as the two were hoping to win the million dollars not only to become the fourth all-female team to win but also to put themselves in a better financial situation after being laid off from their jobs due to the COVID-19 pandemic.

    They mostly finished near the top of the pack and won the two legs leading into the finale, easily securing them a spot in the final three. Raquel & Cayla led for most of the final leg but struggled at the final memory task, in which they made more mistakes and allowed the closely trailing Kim & Penn to overtake them, leading to the flight attendants finishing in 2nd place.
